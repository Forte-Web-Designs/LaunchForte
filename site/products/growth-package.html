What's Included

Everything in the build

Customer Research and Targeting

We figure out who your ideal customer is, what they search for, what objections they have, and what makes them buy. This research drives every other decision in the build.

Ad Campaign Setup (Meta and/or Google)

Audience targeting, ad creative, copy, budget allocation, and conversion tracking pixels installed. Campaigns built around the customer research, not guesswork.

Landing Pages and Funnels

Conversion focused pages built specifically for the campaign. Opt in forms, lead magnet delivery if applicable, and a clear path from click to contact.

CRM Pipeline

Contact records, deal stages, tags, lead source tracking, and status visibility. Every lead has a place in the system from the moment they come in.

Speed to Lead Response

Instant SMS and email on every form submission. CRM record created, internal alert sent to your team, source tagged. Under 60 seconds.

Missed Call Text Back

Every missed call triggers an automatic text to the caller within seconds. CRM logged, team notified. They know you're on it before they dial the next company.

Booking Workflow

Calendar connected to CRM with confirmations, reminders, and source attribution. You know which campaign produced which appointment.

Follow Up Sequences

5 email and SMS sequences: new lead nurture, no show re engagement, post appointment, reactivation, and referral ask. All behavior triggered. All connected to the CRM.

Conversion Dashboard

Leads by source, ad spend vs booked appointments, response times, pipeline status. Auto refreshing. You see what's working without pulling reports manually.

Documentation and Handoff

Loom walkthrough of every component. Written documentation covering the full system architecture. 30 day support window.
