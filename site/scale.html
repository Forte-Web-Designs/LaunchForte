Ad Reporting Dashboards

Every Monday morning, someone on your team opens Meta Ads Manager, copies numbers into a spreadsheet, switches to Google Ads, copies more numbers, tries to line up the columns, double-checks the date ranges, and two hours later has a report that's already outdated. If you manage client accounts, multiply that by every client. That's 5-10 hours a week assembling data that should assemble itself. We pull ad data from Meta and Google automatically, combine the metrics, and deliver formatted reports on a schedule. The dashboard updates itself. Reports land in inboxes on time. Nobody touches a spreadsheet. And here's the part that actually matters: the best thing you can do with your marketing is take your ego out of the picture. You might hate an ad. If the data says it converts, you run it. A $5 lead that doesn't close is worth less than a $45 lead that turns into a $20,000 deal. You can only see that if you're tracking the full picture.

●

Content Repurposing Pipeline

You spend 2 hours creating a video. It gets posted once and forgotten. Meanwhile, that single video has enough material for 10-15 social posts, a newsletter draft, and short-form clips. But extracting all of that manually takes another 5 hours you don't have. So it dies after one post. We build a pipeline where you submit one piece of content, a video, podcast episode, or blog post, and AI automatically generates platform-specific posts for LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, and X. It transcribes the content, pulls key points, writes captions and hashtags optimized for each platform, and queues everything for your approval before publishing. Here's where it gets strategic: repurpose to TikTok first. TikTok shows every video to 100-1,000 people even with zero followers. Videos that get traction organically become your paid ads. You let the platform tell you what resonates before you spend a dollar. Content repurposing stops being about "staying visible" and becomes a direct lead generation strategy.

●

Social Media Scheduling

You write three posts. You open LinkedIn, paste, format, post. Switch to Instagram, reformat, post. Switch to Facebook, paste again, post. Twenty minutes gone for three posts. Do that 3-5 times a week and you're spending 1-2 hours just copying and pasting content you already wrote. If you manage content for clients, multiply that by every account. We build a content calendar in Google Sheets or Airtable that feeds an automated workflow. You fill in the date, platform, caption, and image. The system reads it and posts on schedule. AI generates caption suggestions if you want them. Images get resized for each platform's specs automatically. When a post goes live, the status updates in your calendar so you always know what's been published. You write once. It publishes everywhere.

●

Customer Support Routing

A customer emails about a billing issue. It sits in a shared inbox for 6 hours because nobody knew it was their job to answer it. A second customer submits a form about a broken feature. It gets buried under the billing thread. By the time someone responds, both customers are frustrated and one has already posted a 1-star review. We build a system where incoming support requests get AI-categorized (billing, technical, general, urgent), auto-responded with relevant help content or FAQ answers, and routed to the right team member based on category. Urgent issues get flagged and escalated immediately. Everything is logged in a tracker with response time metrics. Your customers get a fast, relevant response. Your team stops playing inbox roulette. For higher volume, the system drafts first-response suggestions for agents to review and send, runs sentiment analysis to flag frustrated customers, and sends satisfaction surveys after resolution.

●

Recruiting Pipeline

A recruiter gets 200 resumes for one role. They open each one, skim it for 30 seconds, decide if it's a fit, move the good ones to a folder, email the top 10, and spend the next two days coordinating interview times over email. That's 8-10 hours per role just on screening and scheduling. If you're filling 5 roles at once, that's a full-time job that produces zero revenue. We build an AI-powered pipeline that reads incoming resumes, scores them against your job requirements, routes qualified candidates to your recruiter with a summary and score, and archives the rest. High-score candidates trigger instant notifications. Every applicant gets an auto-acknowledgment. For teams doing active sourcing, we configure Apollo for candidate searches and build outreach sequences to passive candidates. Interview scheduling runs on autopilot: candidate hits the right pipeline stage, scheduling link goes out, calendar books, confirmation and reminders fire automatically.

●

E-Commerce Automation

A customer adds $120 worth of products to their cart and leaves. Nobody follows up. Another customer orders, and your team doesn't find out until someone checks Shopify 4 hours later. A product drops to 2 units in stock and nobody notices until it's sold out. After someone buys, nothing happens. No thank-you sequence, no review request, no upsell. Every one of these is revenue leaking out of a business that's already generating sales. We automate the core operations of your store. Abandoned cart detection waits an hour, checks if they came back, and if not, sends a personalized recovery email with their exact cart contents and a direct checkout link. A follow-up goes out 24 hours later if they still haven't purchased. Your team gets notified on every new order. Low inventory items get flagged before they sell out. After purchase, a follow-up sequence handles shipping updates, delivery confirmation, review requests, and customer tagging based on purchase behavior. The math on abandoned cart recovery alone makes this worth it: recovering 15% of abandoned carts at $75 average order value from 500 abandoned carts per month is $5,625 in recovered revenue every month.
