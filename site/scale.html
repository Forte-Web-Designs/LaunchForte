What's Included
●

Ad Reporting Dashboards

Every Monday someone copies numbers from Meta Ads Manager into a spreadsheet, switches to Google Ads, copies more, and two hours later has a report that's already outdated. We pull the data automatically, combine metrics, and deliver formatted reports on a schedule. Nobody touches a spreadsheet.

●

Content Repurposing Pipeline

You spend 2 hours on a video that gets posted once and forgotten. That video has enough material for 10-15 social posts and a newsletter draft. We build a pipeline where you submit one piece of content and AI generates platform-specific posts, captions, and hashtags, queued for your approval before publishing.

●

Social Media Scheduling

You write three posts, then spend 20 minutes copying and pasting across LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook. Multiply that by 3-5 times a week. We build a content calendar that feeds an automated workflow. You fill in the posts. The system publishes everywhere on schedule.

●

Customer Support Routing

A customer emails about billing. It sits in a shared inbox for 6 hours because nobody knew it was their job. We build a system where incoming requests get AI-categorized, auto-responded with relevant help content, and routed to the right team member. Urgent issues get flagged instantly.

●

Recruiting Pipeline

A recruiter gets 200 resumes and spends 8-10 hours per role on screening and scheduling. We build an AI pipeline that scores resumes against your requirements, routes qualified candidates to your team, and automates interview scheduling. No more manual resume sorting.

●

E-Commerce Automation

A customer abandons $120 in their cart and nobody follows up. A product drops to 2 units and nobody notices. After purchase, nothing happens. We automate cart recovery, inventory alerts, order notifications, and post-purchase sequences. Recovering 15% of abandoned carts at $75 average order from 500/month is $5,625 in recovered revenue.

Ongoing Management

Most Scale-stage clients don't want to run these systems themselves. That's the whole point. We offer monthly retainers for ongoing management, optimization, and expansion. You get a dedicated point of contact, regular reporting, and the peace of mind that someone is watching the systems, tuning what's working, fixing what breaks, and building what's next. Retainers start at $1,000/month depending on scope.
