What's Included
●
Paid Traffic and First Leads
The ad's only job is to stop the scroll and sell the click. The landing page sells the product. We build Meta and Google campaigns with targeting, copy, and a comment engagement strategy that converts fence-sitters and lowers your ad costs. For local businesses, we optimize your Google Business Profile so you show up in search.
●
Sales Funnel Automation
People visit your site and leave because there's no system between "someone clicked" and "someone paid." We build the full path: opt-in, lead magnet delivery, nurture sequence, offer page, and booking or payment. Every step tracked so you know where people drop off.
●
Email and SMS Nurture Sequences
When someone opts in, what happens next? For most businesses, nothing. The second dollar is always cheaper than the first, but nobody's talking to the people who already raised their hand. We build automated sequences that follow up, warm leads back up, and convert without you touching anything.
●
Booking System and Conversion Tracking
You're running Meta ads but you can't tell which ones actually book appointments. We configure scheduling, install Meta Pixel, connect server-side tracking, and map conversion events so you see the full picture from ad click to booked call.
