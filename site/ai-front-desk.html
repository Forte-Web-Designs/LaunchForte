The Problem

Most service businesses miss more calls than they answer. The ones that come in after hours, during lunch, when the team is with another customer... those calls don't leave voicemails. They call the next company on the list.

Industry data puts the number somewhere around 60% of inbound calls going unanswered for small businesses. Each one of those is a potential customer who found you, picked up the phone, and got nothing. For home services companies, legal firms, medical offices, and real estate teams, a single missed call can represent thousands of dollars in lost revenue.

Hiring a receptionist solves part of it. But a full-time receptionist costs $35,000-$55,000 per year before benefits, doesn't work nights or weekends, and still only handles one call at a time.

What AI Front Desk Does

AI Front Desk is a fully configured AI phone system that answers every inbound call to your business, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. It sounds natural. It understands context. It captures the caller's information, answers common questions, and routes urgent calls to the right person.

When a call comes in:

  • The AI answers within 2 rings using a voice trained on your business
  • It greets the caller by your business name and asks how it can help
  • It handles common questions (hours, services, pricing, location, availability)
  • It captures the caller's name, phone number, reason for calling, and urgency level
  • It logs everything to your CRM automatically
  • If the caller needs a human, it routes to the right team member or takes a message
  • After the call, it triggers follow-up workflows (text confirmation, email notification to your team, CRM pipeline update)

Your team gets notified in real time. The caller gets a professional experience. Nothing gets lost.

How It Works

The system runs on four layers:

☎
Voice Layer
Retell AI handles the actual phone conversation. Natural-sounding voice, real-time responses, handles interruptions and multi-turn conversations. Not a phone tree. Not "press 1 for sales." An actual conversational AI.
⚙
Conversation Design
Voiceflow manages the conversation logic. What questions to ask, how to handle different scenarios, what to say when someone asks something unexpected, when to escalate to a human. This is custom-built around your specific business.
✉
Communication Layer
Twilio handles the phone infrastructure. Dedicated business phone number (or port your existing number), call routing, SMS confirmation messages to callers, voicemail fallback if needed.
⚡
Automation Layer
n8n connects everything to your existing tools. CRM updates, email notifications, SMS follow-ups, calendar bookings, Slack alerts... whatever your workflow needs. This is the piece that makes it operational instead of just a gimmick.
