Growth Infrastructure

Every business is at one of four stages.
The stage determines what to build.

Most businesses don't have a growth problem. They have a systems problem. Disconnected tools, manual follow-up, leads leaking out the back door. We build the infrastructure that fixes it.

Stage 1 - Foundation

Your tools don't talk to each other. We fix that.

You are paying for tools you never configured. Leads come in by email and get copy-pasted into a spreadsheet. Your CRM is empty or nonexistent. Your booking link goes to a form nobody monitors. We build the plumbing that connects everything so nothing leaks and nothing gets lost.

CRM and Pipeline Setup

You have HubSpot or GHL but nobody set it up. No pipeline, no stages, no contacts flowing through. We build the operating system your sales process runs on.

Payments and Contracts

A customer pays through Stripe. Nothing happens. No CRM tag, no team notification, no receipt. We connect payments to everything else so money moves and the system responds.

Email and Scheduling

Emails hitting spam, booking tools disconnected, intake forms going nowhere. We fix deliverability, connect scheduling to your CRM, and route submissions where they need to go.

Tracking and Attribution

You are spending on ads and can't tell which ones work. We install tracking so every lead source is identifiable and every dollar is accountable.

Workflow Automation

Someone checks a spreadsheet, sends emails based on what they see, and logs what they did. Every week. That entire loop can run itself.

Client Onboarding Automation

Every new client gets a welcome email, intake form, scheduling link, and project setup automatically. No more chasing credentials or forgetting to follow up.

Not sure where to start? Book a Foundation Review. We review your setup, identify the biggest opportunities, and give you a prioritized roadmap.

Stage 2 - Launch

Infrastructure is ready. Now fill it.

You have a CRM with zero contacts in it. No ads running, no funnel converting, no sequences following up. The system is built but nobody is in it. We get your first campaigns live and revenue flowing through the infrastructure.

Paid Traffic Setup

Most businesses try to sell in the ad. The ad's only job is to stop the scroll and sell the click. Meta and Google campaigns built from scratch with audience targeting, creative direction, and budget structure.

Sales Funnels

People visit your site and leave. There is no system connecting traffic to revenue. The funnel builds the entire path from click to customer, and every step is tracked so you know where people drop off.

Email and SMS Sequences

The second dollar is always cheaper than the first. Most businesses spend everything on acquisition and nothing on the people who already trust them enough to pay. Automated sequences fix that.

Booking and Conversion Tracking

You are running Meta ads but can't tell which ones actually book appointments. We connect your scheduling tool, install server-side tracking, and map every conversion from ad click to booked call.

Most clients move here right after a Foundation build. Book a call and we'll scope what your launch looks like.

Stage 3 - Growth

One channel is working. We add more.

If that one channel goes down, your pipeline goes dry overnight. We add channels so you are pulling leads from multiple directions. Inbound captures people already looking for you. Outbound goes and finds new ones. AI handles the volume around the clock.

Outbound Cold Email

270 to 360 emails per day, 15 to 50 meetings per month. Only makes sense for $3,000+ deal sizes. Prospect scraping, AI personalization, automated sequences, reply routing to CRM.

AI Voice Agent

Every missed call is a potential customer going to your competitor. AI answers calls 24/7, qualifies leads, books appointments, and routes complex calls to your team.

Inbound Lead Capture

A lead fills out your form at 2 PM. Nobody sees it until 5 PM. By then, they already talked to your competitor. Instant notification, auto-response, everything flowing into CRM.

Review Automation

You have 12 Google reviews. Your competitor has 87. Guess who gets the call. Happy customers routed to Google, unhappy ones to a private form so you can fix it first.

Dispatch and Assignment

A job comes in. The system ranks the best matches, sends outreach in priority order, confirms the assignment. Replaces manual searching and emailing.

Meta Lead Ads to CRM

Leads from Meta ad forms are not making it to your CRM, are not getting followed up, and are not being tracked. We connect the ad platform so every lead gets instant follow-up.

WhatsApp/Chat Intake Bot

Someone lands on your site at 9 PM with a question. No chat, no bot, no way to get an answer. They leave. This routes visitors to the right path and captures every inquiry.

AI Document Search

Your most important information is buried in PDFs nobody reads and SOPs on one person's desktop. AI-powered search where your team asks questions in plain English and gets answers.

Book a call and we'll identify which channels will move the needle fastest for your business.

Stage 4 - Scale

Everything is running. Make it visible and hands-off.

You can't tell which channel has the best ROI because the data lives in five different tabs. Content takes 10 hours a week. Support tickets are piling up and nobody knows whose job it is to answer them. We add the reporting, automation, and support systems that let you grow without adding headcount.

Ad Reporting Dashboards

Someone copies numbers from Meta Ads Manager into a spreadsheet every Monday. Two hours later the report is already outdated. We pull it all into one dashboard automatically.

Content Repurposing

One long-form piece turns into 10 to 15 social posts, a newsletter draft, and short-form clips. Test on TikTok first, take the winners to paid ads. AI writes, automation distributes.

Support Routing

A customer emails about a billing issue. It sits in a shared inbox for 6 hours. AI categorizes, auto-responds with help content, and routes to the right person instantly.

Recruiting Pipeline

200 resumes per role, 8 to 10 hours screening and scheduling per hire. AI screens, scores against requirements, routes qualified matches, and books interviews.

Social Media Scheduling

You write three posts, then spend 20 minutes copying and pasting across LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook. A content calendar feeds an automated workflow that posts on schedule.

E-Commerce Automation

Abandoned carts with no follow-up, out-of-stock products nobody noticed, zero post-purchase sequences. Every one of these is revenue leaking out. We plug the holes.
